    For this project, we present a design and development plan for two games with the same thematic.
    One board game and one digital game.

    We needed to choose between two topics:
    1. Affective Music Interaction;
    2. Facing a phobia;

    And the requirements for the digital game were: (Should include)

    Challenge

    Kinect

    Bycicle

    Biofeedback

    Oculus Rift

    The aim of our project is to build two games for different platforms (board and digital) but with a coherent visual language in both and also keeping the same story base.
    The game proposal demanded that we incorporated a bycicle and a biofeedback system on the game mechanics, and that we create the game around a phobia context - the Nyctophobia and Lygophobia chosen by the team.

    Phobia

    We were based on the Nyctophobia - not a fear of darkness itself, but a fear of the possible or imagined dangers that are concealed within darkness - and Lygophobia, the fear of being in dark places or darkness.

    "You are dreaming and in your dream you are woken up by a firefly in a dark forest. Now you have to find the glade and help the firefly find his friends, guiding him to the glade and lighting up the forest."

    Games

    "An emmersive experience
    to challenge your fear."

    Boardgame

    Help at least 5 fireflies reach the glade by moving through an unknown path. The boards are facing down and you can only see them when you go forward. Choose the board you want, roll the anxiety dice and move accordingly. Watch out for the obstacles and be sure to pick the right path to the glade.
